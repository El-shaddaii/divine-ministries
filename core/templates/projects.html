Our Projects

Divine Ministries serves vulnerable families, children, elderly individuals, and communities through practical compassion, spiritual encouragement, and real action.

🍚 Food Distribution

Helping families facing hardship with essential food supplies such as rice, maize flour, cooking oil, beans, and other basic groceries.

🧸 Children Support

Supporting orphaned and vulnerable children with food, clothing, toys, school supplies, and care that reminds them they are loved and not forgotten.

👵 Elderly Care

Providing dignity and care for elderly men and women through food, clothing, blankets, bedding, and basic support for daily living.

✝️ Spiritual Support

Beyond meeting physical needs, we share the greatest gift of all—the Gospel of Jesus Christ. Through visits to schools, youth groups, churches, and communities, we bring hope, encouragement, and the life-changing message of salvation.

Be Part of the Mission

Your support helps us reach families, children, elderly people, and communities with practical care, encouragement, and the hope of Christ.
